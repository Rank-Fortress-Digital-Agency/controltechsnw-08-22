What are the Benefits of a Furnace Tune-Up?


Winter is coming, and with it, the need for furnace tune-ups. A good furnace tune-up can help your furnace run more efficiently, saving you money on your energy bills. It can also help to prevent expensive repairs down the road. Here’s what you need to know about the benefits of a furnace tune-up.

If you’re not sure how often to schedule a furnace tune-up, check your owner’s manual or ask your HVAC contractor.

Generally, it’s recommended that furnaces be tuned up at least once a year, preferably in the fall before cold weather sets in. During a furnace tune-up, a technician will clean and inspect your furnace and make any necessary adjustments. This may include lubricating moving parts, checking belts and fans, testing safety controls, and more.
